Welcome to our application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect user information within our application.

1. Introduction

Our application is designed to manage and monitor employee attendance and location in real time. By using our application, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect user data such as name, last name, worker ID, and contact details.
  • Location Data: We collect real-time location data to verify worker attendance at designated checkpoints.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how users interact with the application, including features used and frequency of use.
  • Device Information: We collect data about the device used to access our app, such as model, operating system, and unique identifiers.

3. How We Use the Information

  • Attendance Verification: Location data is used to confirm attendance at assigned work areas.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: We track locations to ensure employees remain within permitted zones during their shifts.
  • Report Generation: The data collected helps generate reports on staff efficiency and workplace security.
  • Notifications and Alerts: Automated alerts are sent when a worker leaves a designated area.

4. How We Share the Information

  • With the User’s Company: Data is shared with the employer to manage attendance and location tracking.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We work with service providers under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With Legal Authorities: Data may be disclosed if required by law or to comply with legal processes.

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, modification, or loss.

6. Data Retention

We retain collected data as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. User Rights

  • Access: Users can request access to their personal data.
  • Correction: Users can request corrections to inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Users can request deletion of their personal data under certain conditions.
  • Processing Restriction: Users may request restrictions on data processing.

8. Consent and Preferences

Users can manage app permissions and privacy settings through the application’s settings.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or other appropriate means.
